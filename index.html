Hi there!

I am an interaction design professional with a year of experience spanning UI/UX Design, Web Design, Front-End Development and Graphic Design.

I specialize in developing user interfaces and experiences on mobile devices, to full-size desktops in order to provide an optimal experience to users. I am proficient in the Adobe Creative Suite of programs, including Illustrator, Photoshop and AfterEffects. I also have solid skills in HTML5, CSS, jQuery and JavaScript.

In my most recent project as a freelance front-end developer, I iterated effective responsive design solutions for a non-responsive web application and re-designed user interfaces through wireframes and prototypes. I successfully delivered HTML5, CSS3 and jQuery front-end code. I hope to continue my growth from being a code-savvy designer to a full front-end developer with a great understanding of design elements.

At PostBeyond, I wear multiple hats on. As a graphic designer, I create visual assets for our marketing team and sale team. As a UI designer, I suggest UI and better UX for our product and visualize it with our development team. Also, as a web designer, I manage and design our website with our marketing team.

I design/develop things to help people to get things done. Let's build something awesome together!
