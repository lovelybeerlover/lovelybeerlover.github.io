    UI/UX Design Concept Pitch

    A concept pitch for a company who is making a product for front line employees at retail stores and food industry.











    Concept

    The main concept of the app is to enble the front line workers who work on shift base to easily swap their shifts and comunicate each others without leaving the platform. Instead of goign back and forth between a calendar app and messanger apps, users can check their own schedules on the platform, send and swap them in the platform. Users can easily see the brief weekely availability on user profile and the platform automatically highlights the schedule in the chat.
